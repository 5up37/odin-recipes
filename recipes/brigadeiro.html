Brigadeiro

Description

A delicious chocolate ball with little chocolate crips, a dish origineted in Brazil

The quantity is variable(can serve as many people you want,so it's very good for parties) and very easy, fast to prepare

Ingredients

  • 1 box of condesed milk
  • 4 spoons of crushed chocolate
  • 1 spoon of butter without salt
  • chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. On a pan, add the condesed milk, the butter and the crushed chocolate

  2. Cook and mix until the brigadeiro begins to unstick of the pan

  3. Let it cold and make small balls putting chocolate sprinkles

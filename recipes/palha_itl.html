Palha Italiana

Description

A delicious dish from Brazil that contains some maisena sorrowded by brigadeiro and with refined sugar above

The quantity is variable(can serve as many people you want) and very easy, fast to prepare

Ingredients

  • 1 box of condesed milk
  • 8 spoons of crushed chocolate
  • 1/2 spoon of butter
  • 1 packge od maisena

Steps

  1. Crush the maisena

  2. Do brigadeiro with the condesed milk,the butter and the chocolate

  3. Mix the brigadeiro with the maisena

  4. Put the mass on an unstich surface

  5. Open the mass, bashing with your hands

  6. Let it cold and cut in small squares
